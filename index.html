Our Story

BUILT ON
A DECADE OF
EXPERTISE

Founded in 2026 and headquartered in Lucknow, Uttar Pradesh, Track and Field Infra Pvt. Ltd. was established with a clear purpose — to elevate the standards of quality, durability, and professionalism in India's sports infrastructure industry.

The company is led by professionals with over a decade of hands-on experience in the sports infrastructure sector — athletics tracks, football grounds, multisport facilities, and sports development projects across India. Throughout their journey, they observed a critical gap: many projects were executed with a primary focus on commercial gains rather than long-term quality, athlete safety, and performance standards.

Recognising this, the founders built Track & Field Infra on a commitment to quality workmanship, technical excellence, transparency, and long-term value. As India prepares for the 2030 Commonwealth Games and future Olympic ambitions, we aim to be the company that builds the foundations for future champions.
