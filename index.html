Who We Are

CRAFTING

SPORTS TURF

Stadias is a leading sports infrastructure company in India, specialising in the design, supply, installation, and maintenance of high-performance artificial turf and sports courts. From grassroots academies to professional-grade pitches, we deliver surfaces built for peak performance.

Our certified team of engineers and installation experts has successfully completed 300+ projects across football, hockey, cricket, badminton, basketball, tennis, and multi-sport facilities — trusted by schools, sports clubs, corporates, and government bodies nationwide.
