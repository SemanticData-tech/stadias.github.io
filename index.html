How It Started

THE STORY
BEHIND
TRACK & FIELD

Track and Field Infra Pvt. Ltd. was born from a simple yet powerful observation: India has no shortage of athletic talent, but it suffers a critical shortage of quality sporting surfaces. Founded in Lucknow in 2026, we set out to change that — one field at a time.

Our founding team brought together civil engineering expertise and a deep passion for sport. We saw schools, academies, and institutions struggling with substandard or non-existent sports infrastructure — and decided to build a company that could deliver world-class surfaces at Indian scales, for Indian conditions, with Indian dedication.

Our Purpose

VISION & MISSION

Vision

To Become India's Most Trusted Name in Sports Infrastructure

We envision an India where every school, academy, and sporting institution has access to professional-grade sports surfaces — surfaces that don't limit athletic potential but rather unleash it. Track & Field Infra aims to be the company that makes that vision a reality across every state and district.

Mission

Building Ground for Greatness, One Surface at a Time

Our mission is to deliver world-class sports infrastructure with uncompromising quality, on-time execution, and post-installation support. We commit to using premium materials, certified construction methods, and transparent processes — so every client receives a surface that performs for decades and nurtures generations of athletes.

Core Values

Quality, Integrity & Athletic Legacy

Every surface we build carries our name and reputation. We operate with complete transparency in materials sourcing, pricing, and timelines. We believe sports infrastructure is not just construction — it is the foundation of athletic legacy, and we treat every project with that gravitas.

Why Choose Track & Field Infra

WHAT SETS US APART

01

Specialized Expertise

We focus exclusively on sports infrastructure — not general construction. That singular focus means deeper knowledge, better material sourcing, and superior execution quality for every track, turf, and court we build.

02

Certified Materials

We only use certified, internationally tested surface materials — synthetic tracks that meet IAAF performance standards, FIFA-quality artificial grass, and durable interlocking court tiles that perform in Indian climate conditions.

03

On-Time, Every Time

Sports season schedules don't move. We understand the pressure of deadlines — whether it's a school sports day or a tournament deadline — and have built our entire project management system around on-time delivery.

04

Post-Installation Support

We don't disappear after handover. Our Annual Maintenance Contracts (AMC) cover full surface upkeep, wear assessment, infill replenishment, and emergency repairs — keeping your investment performing at peak for years.

05

Transparent Pricing

No hidden costs, no surprises. We provide detailed line-item quotations covering materials, civil work, logistics, and installation so clients understand exactly what they're getting — and getting what they paid for.

06

Pan-India Execution

From Delhi schools to Manipur grounds — we've built facilities across the length and breadth of India. Our logistics, vendor network, and site management capabilities handle projects in remote and urban locations alike.

What You Get

When you work with Track & Field Infra, here's exactly what every client receives — from first consultation to final handover and beyond.

Our engineers visit your site, evaluate soil condition, drainage, dimensions, and usage requirements — free of charge before any quotation is made.

Custom Design Layout

A professionally drawn layout plan tailored to your available space, sport requirements, and budget — with 2D/3D visualization so you know exactly what you'll get.

On-Time Delivery Guarantee
